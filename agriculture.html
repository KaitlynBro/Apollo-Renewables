Why Solar?

Reduce Delivery Fees

Delivery and transmission fees often make up 50% or more of our electricity bills. Solar energy is generated on-site and can help reduce fixed costs on your bill.

Sustainability

Sustainability is at the core of every thing we do. Current means of energy production are costly and outdated. It's time to redefine how we harvest our energy while maintaining a sustainable relationship with our environment.

Reduce Expenses

Solar panels can save your farm thousands of dollars a year. This money is better spent on your own farm rather than lining the utility company's pockets.

Put your unused space to work!

Take advantage of your unused land or barn roof with solar. Your solar panels will work sunrise till sunset to harvest energy and save you thousands
