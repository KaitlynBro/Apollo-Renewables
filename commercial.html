Why Solar?

Reduce Expenses

Solar panels can save your business thousands of dollars a year. This money is better spent on your own business rather than lining the utility company's pockets.

Eliminate On-Peak Charges

Solar works best when electricity is most expensive. This means a little bit of energy can make a big impact on your bill.

Green Business is Good Business

Going green is a powerful branding tool. Let your customers know of your commitment to sustainability and create a culture of social responsibility within your company.

Put your roof to work!

Solar takes advantage of your otherwise unused roof and transforms it into a powerful money saver. The panels themselves will shield your roof and extend its life.
