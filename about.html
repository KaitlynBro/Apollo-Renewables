Who We Are

Apollo Renewables is Canada's premier solar brokerage. With over a decade of experience in the industry, our firm offers a full turnkey solar solution for your business. From sourcing the best equipment to project management, we will take you from application to installation.

Our mission is simple, to challenge how we do business in Canada through a commitment to sustainable growth.

Our Story

After years of experience in Ontario's booming solar industry, our founders noticed an issue. With so many companies on the market offering different equipment at vastly different prices, it was becoming increasingly difficult for businesses to navigate these muddied waters to ensure they were getting the best deal.

Apollo Renewables was founded in response to this problem. Our team of brokers have years of experience in the solar industry and work with a large network of vetted installers. We will navigate these waters for you to ensure that you are not gouged by overpriced equipment as well as ensure you do not get into business with a small ﬂy by night operation.

Our services are paid for by the installers, meaning there is no additional cost to your business for using a broker.
